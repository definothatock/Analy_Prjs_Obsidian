  Defines By: Actor Relations

	#PvP - has element of playing ***against*** other player
	#PvE - has element of playing ***against*** Computer
	#Single - negation of the above

# Reward Mode
***limited to game internal convection. Exclude all external flows, e.g. topup.***

[[Simple Winning]] - Winning the round / level / game, no other effective rewards

### Positive reward
rewards that creates positive feedback(s), which are not limited to a loop/round/level.

### Social reward
rewards that does not affect the game, but have strong Social value
